Ecology and Evolution PhD Program Beginner’s R Workshop

2 Technical Requirements

All attendees will be required to bring their own laptop and charger. Tablets and/or Chromebooks (or similar) are not recommended. It can have a Windows, Mac, or Linux operating system, but you must have administrative priveleges.

Attendees are also asked to install both R and RStudio in advance of the workshop. Both R and RStudio are updated regularly with both new features and bug fixes, so we require attendees to have R v3.4 or later, and RStudio v1.1.383 or later.
